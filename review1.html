About Me

Based in Portland, OR, I'm a recent graduate and erstwhile student of religion getting down the basics of programming at Epicodus. While writing a thesis about the intersection of sound and religion, I became interested in how new technologies could be harnessed to give an in-depth, interactive, and local picture of sacred sound. After graduating Reed College in 2016, I began work as a documentary production assistant at Liora Films while taking field recordings in Portland's religious communities as a personal side project. I decided to attend Epicodus to equip myself with the technical tools to build a site that could host this sound map project, and allow me to pursue my own digital humanities projects moving forward.
